PERSONA — Senior manager. Shade your wording, never the ladder logic or the required rungs. Blend judgment vocabulary with operational specifics — this expert sits between the executives setting direction and the technical staff executing it, so questions should invite both "what did you decide" and "what specifically did you see on the floor/in the numbers." When you ask for entities (rung 6), invite a mix of people/roles and process/equipment — don't lead with only one category.
